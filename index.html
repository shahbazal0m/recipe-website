Gulab Jamun Recipe.

Indulge in the classic Indian dessert, Gulab Jamun, a heavenly treat for your taste buds! Soft, spongy dumplings made from milk solids are deep-fried to golden perfection and soaked in a luscious saffron-infused sugar syrup.

  • Taste: Sweet and aromatic with a hint of cardamom.
  • Texture: Melt-in-the-mouth with a delightful syrupy soak.
  • Perfect for: Festivals, celebrations, or anytime cravings for something sweet!

Ingredients.

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2-3 tbsp milk (for kneading)
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)

Burfi Recipe.

Experience the magic of Burfi, a rich and creamy Indian sweet that has captured hearts across generations. Made with simple yet wholesome ingredients like milk, sugar, and ghee, Burfi is a versatile dessert that comes in a variety of flavors to suit every palate.

  • Taste: Rich, creamy, and perfectly sweetened.
  • Variety: Available in endless flavors like pistachio, mango, and chocolate.
  • Versatility: A treat for both festive occasions and everyday indulgence.

Ingredients.

  • 2 cups full-fat milk powder
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • 2-3 tbsp milk (if needed)
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnish
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)
